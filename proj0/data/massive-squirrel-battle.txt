Creator of universe: Christina Ilvento, COS 126, Fall 2006

The Universe which I have created is called massivesquirrelbattle. In this
universe, the epic tale of how the Red Ninja Squirrels came to be bitter 
rivals with the Unassuming Grey Squirrels unfolds:

One day, the Red Ninja Squirrel and the Unassuming Grey Squirrel were 
foraging through the plethora of acorns available in their universe. After 
much foraging and sorting, though, neither squirrel found an acorn he felt 
truly worthy of consumption. The plethora had made them gluttonous and picky. 
Then, at the same moment, they both saw THE ONE ACORN, whose green and gold 
glow held such sway over them, that they began to gravitate towards it. But 
just as each approached THE ONE ACORN, they saw each other! Each squirrel was 
determined to have THE ONE ACORN for his own, and so they battled. However, 
THE ONE ACORN in its great wisdom, drifted away during the battle, sensing 
that neither of the greedy squirrels was fit to devour it. After noticing THE 
ONE ACORN's departure, the two squirrels sprang apart, never to forage in the 
same universe again. THE END.

This universe was inspired by the squirrel battles that I have witnessed on 
campus. They are brief encounters after which both squirrels run their 
separate ways. I initially made a universe in which many acorns orbited 
around one squirrel, but it seemed cruel to always leave the acorns just 
out of reach. Then I began experimenting with two-squirrel systems, and 
decided that having the squirrels battle over an acorn would be a challenging 
problem worth simulating. The pictures of the squirrels and acorns were
